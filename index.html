Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Ontek Horizon.

What services do you specialize in?

We specialize in software development, e-commerce solutions, Amazon FBA management, AI integration, digital marketing, and comprehensive business consulting. Our expertise spans the entire digital commerce ecosystem.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple consulting projects may take 2-4 weeks, while full software development projects can range from 3-12 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We serve clients across 25+ countries and have extensive experience with international e-commerce, cross-border Amazon selling, and global market expansion strategies.

What makes Ontek Horizon different?

Our unique combination of technical expertise, business acumen, and hands-on experience in scaling e-commerce businesses sets us apart. We don't just deliver solutions—we partner with you for long-term success.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive ongoing support including maintenance, performance monitoring, updates, and strategic consulting to ensure your digital assets continue to drive growth.
